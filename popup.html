📈 Nate's Stock Tracker

Welcome to Nate's Stock Tracker!

Navigate to TradingView.com and use Ctrl+Shift+Y (Cmd+Shift+Y on Mac) to open the side panel.

The side panel will show you:

  • Real-time stock data
  • Technical indicators
  • Support & resistance levels
  • AI-powered analysis with Claude

First Time Setup:

  1. Click the ⚙️ icon in the side panel
  2. Enter your Anthropic API key
  3. Start analyzing stocks!
